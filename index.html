My Research

My research focuses on machine learning for spatio-temporal analysis and time series analysis. In particular, I am interested in reliable time series forecasting and virtual sensing at unseen locations. I am recently extending the scope of my research to non-spatial domains such as physiological and medical time series, and adding layers of explainability.

You can find a list of my publications here.

Spatio-Temporal

My primary focus is on ML models for spatio-temporal analysis, includig forecasting, exogenous modeling, and virtual sensing. I explore both traditional approaches, e.g., Gaussian Processes and Neural Networks, particularly Graph Deep Learing.

Time Series

I generally extend my research on the design of ML models for Multivariate Time-series, including forecasting, kernel design, dynamical systems and reservoir computing.

Explainable AI

I aim to improve the explainability of deep learning methods and allowing humans to reliably interact with them.

Broader Impact

Applications of my research are diverse and range from scientific to industrial. A few examples include climate, sustainability, energy forecasting and material weathering.
